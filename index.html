Frequently Asked Questions.

What is MiiSI?

MiiSI is an independent Homebrew and gaming news network. We cover Homebrew releases, emulation news, retro gaming culture, and everything happening in the scene. Built by the community, for the community.

Is Homebrew legal?

Homebrew software itself is completely legal. It refers to software created by independent developers. Emulation is also generally legal. What is not legal is downloading ROMs of games you don’t own. MiiSI does not support piracy in any form.

What platforms and topics do you cover?

We cover Android emulation, classic handheld and console Homebrew, custom firmware, RetroAchievements, retro gaming culture, and community news across all major platforms.
